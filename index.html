How I Work

My Process

01

Discovery

Every project begins with understanding your vision, goals, and requirements. I take time to learn about your brand, target audience, and the story you want to tell through visual content.

02

Concept & Strategy

Based on our discovery phase, I develop a comprehensive creative direction and strategy. This includes mood boards, style frames, and detailed project timelines to ensure alignment with your expectations.

03

Creation

The production phase where concepts come to life. Whether it's 3D modeling, animation, or social media content, I maintain open communication with regular updates and opportunities for feedback throughout the process.

04

Refinement

Attention to detail makes the difference between good and exceptional. I refine every element based on your feedback, ensuring the final deliverables exceed expectations and align perfectly with your brand standards.

05

Delivery & Support

Final deliverables are provided in your preferred formats with all necessary assets. For ongoing projects like social media management, I provide continuous support, analytics, and optimization recommendations.
